About Me

Hi, I’m Yadandla Shruthi — a UX Designer passionate about creating inclusive, user-centered digital experiences.

My journey into UX began unexpectedly during my time as a web developer, where I found myself constantly curious about why users interacted with products in certain ways — not just how to build them. This curiosity led me to complete the Google UX Design Certificate, where I mastered the end-to-end design process from user research and wireframing to prototyping and usability testing.

Since completing the program, I've been actively applying these skills to personal projects — conducting user interviews, creating iterative prototypes, and designing accessible interfaces that solve real-world problems. These hands-on experiences have strengthened my ability to translate user needs into intuitive digital solutions.

What I bring is a unique blend of technical and design thinking. My background in web development gives me a practical understanding of what’s feasible to build, which I now pair with a user-centered design approach to create experiences that are both intuitive and implement xperience across the entire UX process—from research to prototyping. As a motivated newcomer, I offer a fresh perspective, a quick learning curve, and the enthusiasm to start contributing from day one.

I'm currently seeking an entry-level UX Design role where I can grow alongside a supportive team, contribute to meaningful projects, and help build products that make a difference.

Let's connect and discuss how I can bring value to your team!
